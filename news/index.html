2022-07-18

Nature Genetics published "Reference genome assemblies reveal the origin and evolution of allohexaploid ". This study releases a reference-quality genome assembly of the hexaploid oat variety "Sanfensan" and genome assemblies of its diploid and tetraploid Avena ancestors, also provides insights into the evolutionary history of allohexaploid oats. Lipeng Kang was involved in the data analysis, good job!
